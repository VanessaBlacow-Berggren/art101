Collection of Games where the Mod that was Added Breaks the Game from its Original Purpose

PONG

This is a game of Pong where the objective is to avoid the paddles. The mod makes the scoring mechanism trigger when the paddles are hit.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird where the mod changes the objective to hit the pipes. To go with the theme I changed the colors to bring to mind a drop of water cooling off something hot.

Minesweeper

Minesweeper but this mod gives you only one change to either survive or hit a mine and die.
